Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for Inaspro+?

+

You can register using your email or Google account. Open the Inaspro+ app, select "Register", fill in the required information, and verify your account through email.

How do I join Fantasy Football?

+

Go to the Fantasy menu, select an available tournament, choose your favorite team, select jersey and shoes, then pay the registration fee. You'll receive confirmation after successful payment.

What is Dana Talang?

+

Dana Talang is a financing program for booking football fields. You can apply for funds to reserve fields without paying a down payment, with a fast and practical process through the app.

How do I watch TV streaming?

+

Open the TV menu in the app, select the match you want to watch, and enjoy live streaming. Make sure your internet connection is stable for optimal viewing experience.

How do I shop in the Marketplace?

+

Go to the Marketplace menu, browse available products, select desired items, add to cart, and checkout. Payment can be made through various available methods.

How do I join MSL Regional Series?

+

Go to More > MSL Regional Series, fill out the registration form completely including field information and contact person. The MSL team will contact you for confirmation.
